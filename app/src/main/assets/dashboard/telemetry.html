Firmware update
Read this first. Flashing IVCU firmware over Bluetooth takes about 13 minutes. During the update: keep the scooter switched on and within range, keep this app in the foreground (the screen stays on), and do not turn the scooter off. If the flash is interrupted the IVCU stays in update mode and can simply be flashed again - it is not bricked. Only flash a .hex that matches your IVCU.
ver2 device (T2/tetra). This scooter flashes every node - display, light module, the front/rear controllers, BMS - through the display block, selecting the target node from the firmware file's header (a 06 e2 handshake). Pick the matching component's .hex and flash as usual; the node is chosen automatically from the file.
